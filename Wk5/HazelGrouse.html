Hazel Grouse

The Hazel Grouse is a member of the grouse family of birds.
They are native to Northern Eurasia and live in dense, damp coniferous woodland.

Facts about the Hazel Grouse

  • 14 - 15 inches long
  • Grey upperparts
  • Brown wings
  • Chestnut flecked white underparts
  • Males have a black throat
  • Females have a shorter crest
  • Diet consists of plant food
  • Nest on the ground and have 3-6 eggs
  • Very high pitched call
