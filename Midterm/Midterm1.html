The Flavor Chip

What does it do?

The Flavor Chip is a revolutionary product that will change the way you eat!
The chip, implanted into your tongue, will allow you to simulate the taste and feel of any food you desire.
Imagine being able to turn cup noodles into restaurant quality ramen.
You will be able to enjoy pizza, burgers, seafood, barbeque, and much more any time you want!

How does it work?

The nervous system is the network of nerve cells and fibers that transmits nerve impulses between parts of the body. It consists of the brain, spinal cord, sensory organs, and nerves.
The nervous system sends information through out the body using electric signals; this is what the Flavor Chip uses as its advantage.
The Flavor Chip is able to modify these very electric signals thus changing the information being sent throughout your body.
This will effectivly allow one to modify the signals present when eating a certain food in order to produce a different signal carrying information for a different feel and flavor.
