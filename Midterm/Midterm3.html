Benefits

Diabetes and Obesity are two rapidly growing epidemics in USA. Many people have a hard time going on diets and/or controlling what they eat. For most people, eating what they like and want makes them happy, but with a diet, they have to struggle eating healthier foods they may not enjoy. However, with the Flavor Chip, these things will be made significantly easier! Using the Flavor Chip, people can make it so that they can enjoy healthier food options while still enjoying the flavor and feel of the foods they love!
